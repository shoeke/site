We believe that you, our customer, comes first.

Our philosophy is centered around giving you the easiest way to file your 1099 and other information return forms.

eFile360, previously known as Online File Taxes, was founded in 2009 as a way to help business owners and other professionals file information return forms. Founders Rajeev and Smita Srivastava knew there was a better way than purchasing bulk paper forms or using inaccessible self‑service platforms, so they developed eFile360 to be the best cost‑effective, self‑service option for businesses. They continue to serve businesses and their personnel to make tax season easier every year.

As one of the top information return e‑filers authorized by the IRS, eFile360 is here to help guide you through the e‑filing process and complete it on your behalf.

E‑filing with eFile360 eliminates the need to download software, fill out and mail paper copies, and stay up-to-date on the latest IRS regulations. eFile360 manages these aspects for you, so all you have to do is create a free account and enter form details, which typically takes just a few minutes.

We are an IRS-authorized e‑file provider.

eFile360 electronically files 1099, 1098, W‑2, and ACA forms on behalf of our customers. We submit 1099, 1098, and ACA forms to the IRS and W‑2 forms to the Social Security Administration (SSA).

We also support Combined Federal and State Filing for participating states.

We are SOC 2 compliant with the AICPA.

eFile360 has completed the System and Organization Controls (SOC) 2 Type 1 compliance audit and has passed without exception according to the American Institute of CPAs (AICPA).
