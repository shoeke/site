services / how it works

E‑file your
information returns in minutes.

We support 1099, 1098, W‑2, and Affordable Care Act forms.

How it Works

With eFile360, you can file 1099, 1098, W‑2, and Affordable Care Act forms in three steps.

  • Sign up for a free eFile360 account
  • Choose your form type and enter or upload your form details
  • Complete your purchase and place your order

You can enter data manually or upload a spreadsheet file to complete a form. Utilizing a spreadsheet file to complete multiple forms simultaneously is called “bulk upload.” We provide our customers with a bulk upload template. You can export your data from accounting software, and copy and paste it into the template. Once you have populated the spreadsheet template, you can use it to upload your data into our system. This is much faster than manually entering data into each form. Our system will check and report if there are errors in the data provided. Once the data is error free, our system will create the necessary forms for you. Once complete, we recommend that you review each form before placing your order. We provide you with a summary and detailed report for this purpose.

Once you complete your order, our team processes your order, which may include e‑filing, printing and mailing forms to recipients, TIN Checking, and e‑delivery.

After we file your forms, you will receive a confirmation email. Once you receive this email, you can log in to your account in order to print and mail a copy of each 1099 form to state or local agencies, as required,and save and print a copy for your records.

Delivery Options

We offer three delivery options: E‑file + Print & Mail, E‑file Only, and Print &  Mail Only.

E‑file + Print & Mail

This is the default filing option in which eFile360 e‑files with the IRS and prints and mails (or e‑delivers) recipient copies. Choose this option to have eFile360 handle all the printing and delivering of your forms.

E‑file Only

In this option, eFile360 only e‑files. Choose this option if you have already provided copies to your recipients and want eFile360 to report your forms to the IRS.

Print & Mail Only

In this option, eFile360 prints and mails recipient copies. We recommend you choose this option if you have already submitted (or will submit) your information to the IRS but want eFile360 to provide copies to their recipients.

Pricing

eFile360 offers volume pricing, which means the more forms you have to file, the cheaper each form costs. However, individual forms are no more than $3.50 for most forms and $4 for ACA forms.

To save money, we recommend purchasing all of your forms in one order, which may mean you will be eligible for volume pricing.

For our default delivery service, which is E-file + Print & Mail, our services include:

  • E-filing federal copy
  • Printing and mailing recipient copy
  • Payer copy report in PDF format
  • Filing confirmation via email
The cost of e-filing is based on the number of forms in your order and the delivery method you choose. TIN Checking is also available for an additional charge. E‑delivery is included in the price.
